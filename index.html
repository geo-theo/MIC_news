Methodology

Useful signals, explainable ranking.

Signal Brief collects public headlines and federal award records on a scheduled GitHub Action. Each story receives a deterministic relevance score based on:

  1. Industry entities — defense primes, agencies, programs, and platforms.
  2. Material events — awards, protests, production, acquisitions, budgets, and export decisions.
  3. Source quality — direct government and established reporting sources receive a modest weight.
  4. Freshness — recent items receive a small lift; no opaque recommendation model is used.

The score is topical relevance only. It is not a prediction, sentiment score, or buy/sell signal.
